Admission Process

  1. Qualify in JEE Main examination
  2. Qualify for JEE Advanced examination (top 2.5 lakh students from JEE Main)
  3. Participate in Joint Seat Allocation Authority (JoSAA) counseling
  4. Selection based on All India Rank (AIR) in JEE Advanced
